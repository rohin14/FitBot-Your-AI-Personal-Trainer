The Problem with Generalized Workouts

Generic workout plans assume everyone responds the same way to exercises, which isn’t the case. These plans often fail to consider individual goals, physical needs, or limitations, leading to ineffective results, frustration, and even injury. Research shows that people are more consistent and motivated with routines tailored to their goals and interests.
